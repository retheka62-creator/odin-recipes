CHICKEN65 RECIPE

History of chicken65:

  • Chicken 65 originated in Chennai (Tamil Nadu), at the famous Buhari Hotel in 1965.
  • It is known for its spicy, tangy flavor and crispy texture.
  • The number “65” refers to the year it was introduced on the menu.

Ingredients:

  1. Boneless chicken pieces
  2. Cornflour, rice flour
  3. Red chili powder
  4. salt
  5. turmeric
  6. Ginger-garlic paste
  7. Curd (yogurt)
  8. Curry leaves, green chilies
  9. Oil for frying
  10. Lemon juice

Procedure:

  1. Marinate chicken with curd, spices, and ginger-garlic paste for at least 1 hour.
  2. Add cornflour and rice flour to make a coating.
  3. Deep fry the chicken till crispy and golden.
  4. In a pan, heat oil, add curry leaves and green chilies.
  5. Toss the fried chicken in this tempering.
  6. Add a squeeze of lemon juice before serving hot
