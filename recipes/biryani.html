Biryani Recipe

Histroy Of briyani:

  • Biryani originated from Persia and was brought to India by the Mughals.
  • It became popular in royal kitchens, especially in Hyderabad, Lucknow, and Delhi.
  • Today, it is a favorite dish across India with many versions like Hyderabadi, Lucknowi, and Dindigul biryani.

Ingredients:

  1. Basmati rice
  2. Chicken or mutton
  3. Onions
  4. tomatoes
  5. Ginger-garlic paste
  6. mint
  7. yogurt
  8. coriander
  9. Garam masala
  10. chili powder
  11. salt
  12. turmeric
  13. Ghee and oil
  14. briyani species
  15. Saffron

preparation procedure:

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Heat the ghee and oil and add briyani species
  4. Fry onions till golden brown.
  5. Cook the marinated chicken partly.
  6. In another pot, cook rice till 70% done.
  7. Layer rice and chicken, add fried onions, mint, and saffron.

  9. Serve hot with raita or salad.
